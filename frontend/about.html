About the platform

Helping students find housing with less stress and more confidence.

Off-Campus Hub is built to make the apartment search easier for students who need safe, affordable, and practical housing near school.

What it does

A simpler way to compare student apartments

The site brings apartment listings into one place so students can quickly check prices, distance from campus, amenities, landlord details, and location information before deciding what to visit.

Instead of asking around blindly or scrolling through scattered posts, students can browse listings in a cleaner and more organized way.

Roommate Finder

Roommate Finder helps students connect with compatible roommates before splitting rent or moving into shared housing.

Set preferences

Share budget, lifestyle, study habits, cleanliness, and preferred locations.

Get better matches

The feature compares preferences so students can find people who fit their living style.

Connect safely

First contact is made easier while keeping student comfort and trust in mind.

Why use it

Benefits for students and landlords

Faster searching

Students can compare apartments without jumping between too many platforms or messages.

Better decisions

Price, distance, amenities, and contact details are shown clearly before a student reaches out.

Useful for landlords

Landlords can add listings and make their available spaces easier for students to discover.

Built for campus life

The experience is focused on student housing needs, not generic property browsing.
